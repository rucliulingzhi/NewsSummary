RUCIRM 项目团队

本网站由中国人民大学信息资源管理学院"基于 HubZero 开源项目二次开发的党员 GIS 信息系统"项目团队制作，由王丹、王应解两位老师指导。从宏观角度出发，结合 GIS 系统，制作老党员路线迁移模型，旨在建立一个老党员相关文献资料数字化，有序化、可视化的新型展示平台。

Produced by the project team of the Party Member GIS Information System based on the secondary development of the HubZero Open Source Project of the School of Information and Resource Management at RUC. It is developed from a macro perspective and combined with a GIS system. Aims to establish a new display platform with digitalized, orderly and visualized documents and documents related to the old party members.
